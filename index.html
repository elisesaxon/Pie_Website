General Pie Info

  • In the 19th Century, pie was a breakfast food.
  • Pie became a holdiay food in the U.S. when the pilgrims had it on their second Thanksgiving celebration.
  • 19% of people in the U.S. prefer apple pie, while 13% like pumpkin and only 9% with a favorite of cherry.
  • Boston cream pie is actually a cake.
  • Key lime pie is Florida's state pie.
  • From 1644 to 1660 in England, eating pie was banned, thanks to Oliver Cromwell, because it was considered a pagan form of pleasure.
